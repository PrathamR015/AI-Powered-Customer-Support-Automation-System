PRICING GUIDE
-------------------
ABC Technologies offers the following pricing plans for our cloud-based business management software:

1. Basic Plan
- Price: Rs.299 per month (billed monthly).
- Target Audience: Small businesses, solo entrepreneurs.
- Features: Core business tracking, single-user access, basic dashboard, email support.
- Limitations: No integrations, no advanced reporting.

2. Professional Plan
- Price: Rs.799 per month (billed monthly).
- Target Audience: Growing businesses.
- Features: Advanced reporting, multi-user access (up to 5 users), software integrations (CRM, Slack, QuickBooks), priority email support.

3. Enterprise Plan
- Price: Rs.1999 per month (billed monthly).
- Target Audience: Large organizations.
- Features: Custom support channel, unlimited users, dedicated account manager, API access, 99.9% uptime SLA guarantees.
