COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT
-----------------------
This document outlines policies regarding high-risk customer requests at ABC Technologies:

1. Refund Policy
- Customers are eligible for a full refund on annual subscriptions if requested within 30 days of the purchase date.
- Refunds requested after 30 days are generally not allowed except under extreme circumstances.
- All refund requests must be routed to the Billing department.
- CRITICAL: All refund requests require explicit review and approval by a human supervisor. They cannot be auto-approved.

2. Subscription Cancellation Policy
- Customers can cancel their subscription at any time.
- Upon cancellation, the subscription remains active until the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are issued for unused days.
- CRITICAL: Subscription cancellation requests require human supervisor review and approval before final confirmation.

3. Account Closure Policy
- Customers can request permanent closure of their account.
- This action is irreversible and will delete all stored business data within 14 business days.
- CRITICAL: Account closure requests must be validated and approved by a human supervisor to prevent accidental data loss.

4. Compensation Policy
- If a customer experiences billing errors or system downtime, support representatives can issue compensation (billing credit) up to a maximum of $50.
- CRITICAL: Any compensation request must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor.

5. Escalation to Management
- If a customer is highly dissatisfied and requests escalation to management, the ticket must be flagged.
- CRITICAL: Escalations to management must be approved by a human supervisor before being assigned to a manager.
